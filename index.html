Shakespeare in virtual reality

In 2017, I spent nearly all of my free time working with a team to bring Hamlet into VR using live motion capture for the TriBeCa film festival in 2017. I specifically worked on the motion capture stream and lobby experience. Check out the video I made!

HyperSpace VR

Following the 2017 TriBeCa premiere of To Be With Hamlet, I joined a small VR research team at NYU Shanghai. Our work primarily focused on using motion caption, VR, and pneumatic pistons to create theme park-like attractions. HyperSpace VR came from our shared love of Flim Cosmos. We wanted to transport guests across to the universe and witness the aw of the great beyond. Images and videos coming soon!

Bananas AR

An ongoing concept of creating an entry-level experience to Augmented Reality. After taking lessons from what didn't work and what did work in my previous VR projects. I realized that the onboarding experience in a relatively new medium has to simple and ideally requires no external help/explanation. I also learned that collaborating in this space can be incredibly challenging. I found that a combination of Figma and Vectary's plugins can help with illustrating new concepts. Click here to see the Figma prototype.
