About Alexis

Hi! I design things, but most importantly, I ask questions. I’m a people-person, and that’s the biggest reason why I love what I do. Whether it’s a user, a stakeholder, or a fellow team member, I incorporate their needs, their perspectives, and their values in everything I do. I’m a strong believer in an iterative process that involves frequent stakeholder visibility, data-driven decision-making, frequent co-creation, and constant user feedback.
‍
In my free time, you can find me cultivating my vast collection of indoor plants, and tinkering with vintage electronics.

My values

People matter

The first most important person in any project is the user. A close second place is my teammates. Close collaboration and a user-centered focus are the secret ingredients to any successful product.

Value beyond wires

I believe my role as a designer reaches far beyond screens. I see my role as to advocate for the user, test, problem-solve, and encourage transparency and user centricity through everything I do.

Strong process

Even when unexpected problems pop up (they always do) you’ll find me striving for a double diamond approach of learning, iterating, and delivering value.
